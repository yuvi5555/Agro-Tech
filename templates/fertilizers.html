Popular Fertilizers in India

Urea

Urea is a nitrogen-rich fertilizer commonly used for wheat, rice, and maize.

Di-Ammonium Phosphate (DAP)

DAP is widely used for its phosphorus content and is ideal for cereals and vegetables.

Potash

Potash is essential for crops like sugarcane, potatoes, and cotton for improving quality.

Organic Compost

Enrich your soil with compost for sustainable farming and better crop growth.
